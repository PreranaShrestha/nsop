Resources for Newcomers

Moving to British Columbia? We're here to help you settle in, find your community, and thrive in your new home.

🏠

Housing & Settlement

Find housing resources, rental guides, and settlement services in Kelowna and the Okanagan.

 💼

Employment & Careers

Job boards, resume help, credential recognition, and networking with Nepali professionals in BC.

 🏥

Healthcare & MSP

BC Medical Services Plan enrollment, finding family doctors, and healthcare navigation support.

 📚

Education & Schools

School enrollment, post-secondary options, English language programs, and credential assessment.

 🚗

Driving & Transportation

BC driver's license conversion, ICBC information, and public transit guides for newcomers.

 🏦

Banking & Finance

Opening bank accounts, building credit, tax filing help, and financial literacy resources.

 ⚖️

Legal & Immigration

Immigration pathways, PR applications, citizenship, and legal aid resources in BC.

 💬

Community Support

Mental health resources, community support groups, and the NCSBC Slack community for newcomers.

Our Story

25+ Years of Community, Culture & Connection

NCSBC was established in 1998 with the primary purpose of promoting unique Nepali culture and historical identity while helping fellow community members build a prosperous and meaningful future.

From organizing Dashain and Tihar celebrations to supporting newcomers with settlement resources, we have been the backbone of the Nepali community in British Columbia for over two decades.
